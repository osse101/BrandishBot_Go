[info:farming]
Farming: Passive rewards! Wait (1hr-1wk) then !harvest. Rewards increase with time. Spoilage > 2wks! 5hr+ wait = Farmer XP. More: !info harvest, !info compost

[info:harvest]
Harvest: Use !harvest to collect. Min wait 1hr. Max rewards ~1wk. >2wks = Spoiled. >5hrs wait yields Farmer XP.

[info:farmer]
Farmer Job: Earn XP by harvesting crops >5hrs old. Level up to improve yield/perks. Check !job-progress.

[info:compost]
Compost: Rare drops from harvesting. Contains loot/mats. Upgrade Compost Bin in Progression Tree for better results.
